Composite priority score = unweighted mean of four min-max-normalised indicators per LGA: ACLED political-violence events (2020-2026), IOM DTM Round 50 IDP individuals, OCHA JIAF 2026 Education sector severity (1-5), and the inverse of the HeiGIT 5 km school-age accessibility share. LGAs are tercile-classified into high (red), medium (orange) and low (yellow). The OCHA JIAF Education layer is the agreed 2026 humanitarian community estimate and replaces an earlier iMMAP 2019 school list whose Adamawa coverage had a state-wide data ceiling — see docs/adamawa_decision.md.

Sources: ACLED political-violence events (Apr 2026, CC BY-NC-SA 4.0); IOM DTM Round 50 (Oct 2025); OCHA JIAF Nigeria 2026 — Education sector PiN & Severity (2026 HNO); iMMAP / Education Sector school list (Jun 2019, context only); OCHA Nigeria COD-AB (current); UNFPA / NPC Nigeria population projections (2022); HeiGIT Global Education Accessibility Indicators (Feb 2026).
