Composite priority score = unweighted mean of four min-max-normalised indicators per LGA: ACLED political-violence events (2020-2026), IOM DTM Round 50 IDP individuals, percentage of schools listed as closed in the iMMAP 2019 school list, and the inverse of the HeiGIT 5 km school-age accessibility share. LGAs are tercile-classified into high (red), medium (orange) and low (yellow). The 21 Adamawa LGAs are shown as validation needed because the iMMAP source records 0 closed schools in Adamawa, which is a data ceiling rather than a finding.

Sources: ACLED (Apr 2026, CC BY-NC-SA 4.0); IOM DTM Round 50 (Oct 2025); iMMAP / Education Sector school list (Jun 2019); OCHA Nigeria COD-AB (current); UNFPA / NPC Nigeria population projections (2022); HeiGIT Global Education Accessibility Indicators (Feb 2026).
