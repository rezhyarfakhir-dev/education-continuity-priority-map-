How to read this map

Each LGA is scored on four indicators: recorded political violence events since 2020, current displaced population, the OCHA Education Cluster severity score for 2026, and the share of school age children who live more than 5 km from a working school. The four indicators are combined into one number; the top third are red, the middle third orange, the bottom third yellow.

Method: unweighted mean of four min max normalised indicators per LGA, tercile classified across all 65 LGAs. The OCHA JIAF Education layer replaces an earlier iMMAP 2019 school list whose Adamawa coverage had a state wide data ceiling. See docs/adamawa_decision.md for the full story.
